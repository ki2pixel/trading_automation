# How to close my CFD position?

You can close/sell any Open or Pending positions on your CFD account in multiple ways. Here's how:

Tapping on the X button

1.   Go to your portfolio
2.   Navigate to the Open Positions or Pending Orders section, depending on the type of position you want to close
3.   Tap on the **X** button and confirm the close
4.   Confirm the close and finalise the action.

Long tapping on the position

1.   Go to your portfolio
2.   Navigate to the Open Positions or Pending Orders section, depending on the type of position you want to close
3.   Tap and hold on to the position tile for 2 seconds until an action menu appears.
4.   Select **Close position** and finalise the action

Close via Modify

1.   Go to your portfolio
2.   Navigate to the Open Positions or Pending Orders section, depending on the type of position you want to close
3.   Tap and hold on to the position tile for 2 seconds until an action menu appears.
4.   Select **Modify**
5.   Select the closing side of the position
6.   If you hold Buy position, select Sell
7.   If you hold Sell position, select Buy
8.   Drag the slider to the right and tap the **Confirm close** button.
