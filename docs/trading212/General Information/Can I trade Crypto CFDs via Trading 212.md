# Can I trade Crypto CFDs via Trading 212?

Crypto CFDs are available for trading under Trading 212 Markets Ltd. and Trading 212 EU GmbH. You can find information about the applicable margin rates [here](https://www.trading212.com/terms/invest). The availability may depend on your account entity and jurisdiction.

📄 **Note**

Crypto-assets are high-risk, and you could lose all your money. Your capital is at risk. Prices can be volatile, and you may have no protection if things go wrong. You should consider whether you understand how crypto-assets work and whether you can afford to take the high risk of losing your money.
