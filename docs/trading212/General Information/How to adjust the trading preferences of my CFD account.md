# How to adjust the trading preferences of my CFD account?

You can easily customise your trading preferences through the **Account Settings** menu.

Which preferences can I adjust?

*   **Show positions on chart:** When enabled, your open positions will be displayed directly on the trading chart for better visibility and tracking.
*   **Show price alerts on chart:** This option activates alerts when important position-related events occur, such as hitting a Stop Loss or Take Profit level.
*   **Positions Rollover:** If enabled, your open positions will be rolled over to the next trading day instead of closing automatically when the session ends.
