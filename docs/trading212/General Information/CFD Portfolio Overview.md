# CFD Portfolio Overview

The CFD Portfolio has been updated with a cleaner layout and clearer structure, making it easier to view your account value, margin, positions, and pending orders. Asset Allocation is no longer shown, allowing the focus to remain on information most relevant for CFD trading.

📄 **Note**

This update does not change how you trade CFDs.

Where can I see my account value and result?

Your account value and overall result are displayed at the top of the portfolio.

How is the margin shown now?

Margin is displayed in a separate section, away from your positions.

What is the Health indicator?

The Health Indicator is a monitor that tracks your account's financial status. It calculates if you have enough funds to keep your current trades active.

Where can I see my available cash?

Available cash is shown in a dedicated Cash section near the top of the portfolio.

How are positions displayed?

Positions for the same instrument are grouped together in one section. The trade direction is shown in the position title (for example, **Sell · Tesla**).

How are pending orders displayed?

Pending orders follow the same layout as positions and show units and target price. The current price is no longer shown in the pending orders list.

📄 **Note**

New symbols are used to indicate Set limit/stop, Take profit, and Stop loss.
