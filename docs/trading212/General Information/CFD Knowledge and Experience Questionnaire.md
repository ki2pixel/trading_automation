# CFD Knowledge and Experience Questionnaire

When opening a CFD account, you'll need to complete a short questionnaire to assess your trading knowledge and experience. Since CFDs are complex financial products, this step ensures you understand the risks involved before trading.

What happens after the tests?

*   **If you pass the questionnaire:** You can proceed with the account verification process.
*   **If you don’t pass:** Your account will enter a cooldown period, meaning you’ll need to wait before retaking the test. Once the cooldown ends, you can try again.

Can I retake the test?

Yes, you will have the option to watch three educational videos covering key topics from the questionnaire. After completing them, you can retry the test immediately.
