# [Financial Formulas](#/use/financialFormulas?id=financial-formulas)

The financial world is overflowing with ratios and metrics derived from reported financials or real-time price behaviour (e.g. P/E, P/B, ROE, ROCE and so on!). Financial institutions won't always agree on how these ratios and metrics are calculated. Here are the formulas used by SheetsFinance for ratios and metrics available in our add-on:
