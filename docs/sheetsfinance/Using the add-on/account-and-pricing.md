## [SheetsFinance Accounts and Google Accounts](#/use/account-and-pricing?id=sheetsfinance-accounts-and-google-accounts)

A SheetsFinance account is separate from a Google account. In most cases, and for simplicity, the two accounts will share the same email address, but this is not a requirement. **One** SheetsFinance account provides **one** Connection ID which can be used by a single Google Account at any one time (the exception is our Business Team plan which allows managing multiple Connection IDs for a number of users). Swapping the Google Account you use with SheetsFinance is as simple as entering your Connection ID from the alternative Google Account when logged into Google Sheets. This will disconnect you from your previously connected account.

## [On Installation](#/use/account-and-pricing?id=on-installation)

### [Installing from Google Sheets Before Creating a SheetsFinance Account](#/use/account-and-pricing?id=installing-from-google-sheets-before-creating-a-sheetsfinance-account)

If you install SheetsFinance from the Google Workspace Marketplace, either within Google Sheets from 'Get Add-ons' or from the Google Workspace store page, you do not need to create a SheetsFinance account. During the process of installation, a SheetsFinance account is created for you and your **15 day, full access free trial** will begin immediately. This trial is linked to a SheetsFinance account that has been created using your Google Account's email address. To manage your account, including subscribing once your trial is complete, you will need to finalise the creation of your SheetsFinance account by [connecting your account](#/use/account-and-pricing?id=connecting-your-account) and setting a password.

### [Creating a SheetsFinance Account Before Installing the Add-on](#/use/account-and-pricing?id=creating-a-sheetsfinance-account-before-installing-the-add-on)

If you first create a SheetsFinance account by heading to the [SheetsFinance website](https://sheetsfinance.com) and then install the add-on following the Installation Demo on your [Account Dashboard](https://sheetsfinance.com/account), you won't need to complete any further connection process. Once you install the extension it will be automatically connect to your SheetsFinance account. If for any reason the connection doesn't occur automatically, you can manually connect your account by entering your Connection ID by clicking 'Account' from the Extensions dropdown menu in Google Sheets (Extensions > SheetsFinance > Account).

## [Connecting your Account](#/use/account-and-pricing?id=connecting-your-account)

To manage your SheetsFinance subscription you need to have a SheetsFinance account that you can access on our website. If, like most users, you find the extension on the Google Workspace Marketplace and install it before creating a SheetsFinance account on our website, then you will need to follow the connection flow to finalise the creation of a SheetsFinance account and to connect your currently active free trial. This is done by clicking 'Account' from the dropdown menu in Google Sheets (Extensions > SheetsFinance > Account) and clicking 'Connect Account' from the top of the Account modal.

---

## [Free Trial](#/use/account-and-pricing?id=free-trial)

Our 15-day free trial does not require any payment details and provides users with full access to all SheetsFinance features. The trial will begin automatically when you install the extension from the Google Workspace Marketplace. If you have created a SheetsFinance account on our website before installing the extension then your free trial be created in a `pending install` state and will only begin once you have connected your account Google Account.

## [How to Update Payment Details](#/use/account-and-pricing?id=how-to-update-payment-details)

Simply head to your [Account Dashboard](https://sheetsfinance.com/account) and click the 'Settings' tab at the top of the page. From there you can update your payments details. You can also update your payment details when changing (upgrading/downgrading) your subscription from the [Pricing Page](https://sheetsfinance.com/pricing).

## [How to Change Your Subscription](#/use/account-and-pricing?id=how-to-change-your-subscription)

You can change your subscription at any time by heading to the [Pricing Page](https://sheetsfinance.com/pricing) and selecting the new plan you would like to subscribe to.
